## 1. EU/EEA-only configuration

For the Caldera Works Europe production tenant, NovaMind will configure and contractually maintain the following controls before activation:

1. Customer Content, including uploaded documents, extracted text, application records, and tenant-specific indexes, will be stored only in infrastructure located in the European Union or European Economic Area.
2. Production database replicas, object-storage replicas, and disaster-recovery backups containing Customer Content will remain in the European Union or European Economic Area.
3. Processing of Customer Content by the core application and inference-routing services will occur in the European Union or European Economic Area.
4. NovaMind personnel outside the European Union or European Economic Area will not access Customer Content unless Caldera Works Europe gives case-specific written authorization or access is strictly necessary to respond to a confirmed security incident. Any such exceptional access will be logged and handled under the transfer safeguards in the executed Data Processing Addendum.
5. This commitment overrides the proposal's general statement that hosting uses global cloud infrastructure for this specific tenant.

The tenant configuration will be recorded in the final order form as `DATA_REGION=EU_ONLY`. NovaMind will provide a configuration export to the customer service owner after provisioning.
